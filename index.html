About Us

The Qatar Talks is a premier communication platform celebrating 20 years of empowering voices. Join us as we explore the art of eloquence and leadership.

1. About The Qatar Talks

Our Legacy: 20 Years of Empowering Voices

The Qatar Talks is the annual flagship event of the International Competent Communicators (ICC-ONE), an organization founded on the principle of Community Social Responsibility (CSR). Since its inception, this national initiative has evolved from a competition into a vibrant platform that celebrates the art and science of communication.


The journey, made possible by the vision and dedication of our founder, Mr. Mohomed Ikhlas Farid, marks its 20th edition in 2025. Over two decades, The Qatar Talks has proudly noted the benefit to thousands of residents and school children who have seen improvement in their communication and leadership skills.

2. Our Mission: Communicate, Connect, Conquer

Our mission is to nurture eloquence, creativity, and leadership among participants from diverse communities in Qatar. We believe that public speaking is an enduring powerâ€”a vital bridge that connects minds and hearts, empowering individuals to become agents of positive change.

The Qatar Talks is a forum for:

  • Nurturing Skills: Developing essential communication skills, critical thinking, and confidence.
  • Fostering Community: Strengthening bonds, promoting mutual respect, and cultural understanding across different communities to build a more inclusive and informed society.
  • Inspiring Growth: Encouraging the community to embrace the mantra:
"SUCCESS IS NOT FINAL, FAILURE IS NOT FATAL: IT IS THE COURAGE TO CONTINUE THAT COUNTS."

3. Standing at the Crossroads of Tradition and Technology

In a world reshaped by Artificial Intelligence (AI), The Qatar Talks stands firm on the irreplaceable essence of human connection. While technology aids how we draft and prepare speeches, the core of our event is the human warmth, emotion, and authenticity that our participants bring to the stage.



The 2025 Championship

The Qatar Talks Championship 2025 embraces innovation by featuring a blend of diverse forms of expression. We proudly introduce:

  • Tall Tales: A new category celebrating imagination, humor, and the blending of fact and fiction through creative storytelling.
  • Inspirational Talk: A segment dedicated to sharing ideas, stories, and visions that motivate and uplift.

This forum is an opportunity for every participant to share their perspectives with confidence and passion, using their voices to inspire, connect, and transform.

4. Our Gratitude

An event of this magnitude is only possible through the unstinted support of our dedicated team, our multi-national, self-devoted Judges, our various Role Players, and our generous Sponsors and well-wishers who support this annual CSR initiative.

We invite you to join our journey and witness the power of a voice well-trained and a message well-told.
